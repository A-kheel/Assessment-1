What is Euro Truck Simulator 2?

Euro Truck Simulator 2 (ETS2) is a truck driving simulation game developed and published by SCS Software. Released in 2012, the game allows players to drive trucks across a realistic open-world map of Europe, transporting cargo between cities and managing their own trucking business.

Why play Euro Truck Simulator 2?

  • Relaxing Gameplay – Perfect for casual play, long hauls, and enjoying scenic European roads.
  • Customization – Modify trucks with custom paint jobs, tuning parts, and accessories.
  • Multiplayer Mode – Play online via TruckersMP or the official Convoy mode.
  • VR Support – Compatible with virtual reality for an immersive experience.
