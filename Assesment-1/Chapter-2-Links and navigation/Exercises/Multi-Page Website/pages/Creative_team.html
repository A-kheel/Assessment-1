About SCS Software

SCS Software is a Czech game development company known for creating high-quality truck simulation games. They specialize in developing realistic driving experiences, particularly focused on long-haul trucking across Europe and North America. The company has built a strong reputation for regular game updates, extensive modding support, and an engaged player community. Their flagship titles, Euro Truck Simulator 2 and American Truck Simulator, continue to receive DLC expansions, new features, and gameplay improvements years after their release.

Popular Games by SCS Software

  • Euro Truck Simulator 2 (2012) – The most famous game in their lineup, allowing players to drive trucks across Europe.
  • American Truck Simulator (2016) – Similar to ETS2 but set in North America, with a growing map that expands state by state.
  • 18 Wheels of Steel series (2002-2011) – Early truck driving games focused on US trucking.
  • Scania Truck Driving Simulator (2012) – A realistic truck-driving game in collaboration with Scania.
  • Bus Driver (2007) – A public transport simulation game.

Recent Developments

SCS Software continues to expand both ETS2 and ATS, adding new countries, states, trucks, and features. They also work on multiplayer improvements, dynamic weather systems, and new AI behaviors.
