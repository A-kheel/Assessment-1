Features

  • Realistic Truck Driving – Drive licensed trucks from manufacturers like Volvo, Scania, Mercedes-Benz, and DAF.
  • Massive Open World – Explore cities across multiple European countries, including the UK, Germany, France, and Italy.
  • Freight Transport – Take contracts to deliver various types of cargo, from food to construction materials.
  • Business Management – Buy garages, hire drivers, and expand your trucking empire.
  • Realistic Mechanics – Features include fuel consumption, fatigue simulation, fines, and vehicle wear & tear.
  • Mod Support – A large modding community provides custom trucks, maps, and graphics enhancements.
  • DLC Expansions – Various map expansions add countries like Iberia (Spain & Portugal), the Balkans, Scandinavia, and Russia.
